# **App Name**: NeuroQuest

## Core Features:

- Guided Therapeutic Modules: Interactive user interface for engaging with psychological exercises from the Mental Observer (thought logging, labeling), Exposure Mode (ERP missions), and Emotional Regulation (breathing, grounding techniques).
- Gamified Progression System: Core mechanics for earning experience points (XP), advancing through levels, achieving badges, and receiving 'mental combat' feedback to reinforce positive behaviors.
- AI-Powered Adaptive Personalization: An AI tool that analyzes individual user patterns, including types of intrusive thoughts, frequent compulsions, and periods of high anxiety, to dynamically generate personalized therapeutic missions and cognitive coaching suggestions.
- Mental Progress & Statistics Tracking: Visual dashboards displaying a user's therapeutic progress, including resisted compulsions, identified thoughts, completed exposures, and minutes of calmness over time, providing motivational feedback.
- Professional Therapist Dashboard: A secure web panel, implemented with Next.js, allowing psychologists to monitor their patients' progress, assign customized exposure therapy missions, review thought records, and communicate support.
- Safety & Crisis Resources: Provides immediate, easily accessible in-app buttons for crisis intervention and a directory of emergency mental health resources.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to convey calmness and clarity. Primary color: a serene blue (#6EABD6), symbolizing focus and stability. Background color: a very light, desaturated blue (#E6F0F5), providing a clean, spacious canvas. Accent color: a vibrant mint green (#61D6D6), drawing from the suggested 'verde menta' to highlight key actions and achievements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance, aligning with the scientific and structured nature of the therapeutic approach.
- Use conceptual and gamified icons. Examples include icons for achievements (medals, badges), progress indicators (strength bars, shields), and metaphorical elements like 'The Intruso' representing challenging thoughts.
- Employ a minimalist and structured layout to enhance focus. Content should be clearly segmented into modules, presenting information and exercises in an organized, easy-to-digest format without aggressive stimuli.
- Subtle, non-distracting animations for user feedback, such as earning XP, completing missions, or when displaying new insights. Animations should be encouraging and reinforce positive engagement without being overwhelming.
